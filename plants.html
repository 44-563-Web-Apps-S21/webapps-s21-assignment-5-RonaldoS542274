V - VELVET

The velvet plant is an evergreen herbaceous perennial in the Gynura genus, which includes several dozen species, several of which are common houseplants.

Selected species
  • VELVET ROYAL. $ Juneau. Alaska.
  • VELVET GYNURA. $ Lakewood. Calfornia.
  • VELVET PURPLE. Peru.
Cultivation and uses

Velvet Leaf is also used in paper making and for caulking. In a pinch, the soft leaves can be used as a toilet paper substitute.
